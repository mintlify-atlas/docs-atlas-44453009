## Creating an Account

### Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Registration Page">
    Click **"Crear cuenta nueva"** on the login page or navigate directly to the registration page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill In Your Information">
    Provide the following details:
    - **Full Name**: Your complete name
    - **Email**: A valid email address (used for login)
    - **Password**: Minimum 6 characters
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click **"Crear Cuenta"** to submit your registration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
After registration, you'll receive a confirmation message asking you to check your email.
</Note>

### Email Verification

Upon registration, TechStore sends a verification email to activate your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Inbox">
    Look for an email from TechStore (check spam/junk folder if not in inbox).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Activation Link">
    Open the email and click the **"Verify Account"** link.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Account Activated">
    You'll see a success page confirming your account is active.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login">
    Return to TechStore and log in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you don't receive the verification email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
</Tip>

---

## Logging In

Once your account is verified, you can access TechStore:

1. Navigate to the **Login** page
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **"Iniciar Sesión"**

<Note>
If you haven't verified your email, you'll see a message: "Tu cuenta no está activa. Revisa tu correo."
</Note>

---

## Password Recovery

If you forget your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Password Recovery">
    On the login page, click the **"¿Olvidaste tu clave?"** link.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide the email address associated with your account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    You'll receive a password reset link via email.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set New Password">
    Click the link in the email and enter your new password (minimum 6 characters).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Main Storefront

The TechStore homepage displays:

- **Hero Banner**: Featured promotions and call-to-action
- **Featured Products Carousel**: Highlighted products rotating automatically
- **Product Grid**: All available products displayed as cards

### Product Features

### Using Categories

Filter products by category using the category buttons:

1. Locate the **category filter bar** above the product grid
2. Click on a category (e.g., "Laptops", "Smartphones", "Accessories")
3. View products filtered to that category
4. Click **"Todas"** to show all products again

### Search Functionality

Quickly find specific products:

1. Locate the **search bar** in the top section
2. Type your search term (product name or keyword)
3. Results filter automatically as you type
4. Clear the search box to show all products

<Tip>
The search is real-time - you'll see results update as you type!
</Tip>

## Product Details

Click on any product card to view detailed information:

### Product Detail Modal

The product detail view includes:

- **Image Gallery**: Browse multiple product images using carousel controls
- **Product Name**: Full product title
- **Category**: Product classification
- **Price**: Current price in USD
- **Stock Availability**: How many units are available
- **Quantity Selector**: Choose how many units to add

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Product Details">
    Click on any product card in the catalog.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Images">
    Use the left/right arrows to view all product photos.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Quantity">
    Use the **+** and **-** buttons to choose quantity (respects available stock).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to Cart">
    Click **"Agregar al carrito"** to add the item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Shopping Cart

The shopping cart is accessible from the navbar at all times.

### Cart Overview

- **Cart Icon**: Shows the number of items currently in your cart
- **Cart Sidebar**: Slides in from the right when clicked

### Managing Your Cart

<Accordion title="Adding Items to Cart">
  You can add products to your cart in two ways:
  
  1. **Quick Add**: Click "Agregar al carrito" directly from the product card
  2. **Detailed Add**: Open product details, select quantity, then add
  
  Each time you add an item, you'll see a success notification.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Viewing Cart Contents">
  Click the **cart icon** in the navigation bar to open the cart sidebar.
  
  You'll see:
  - List of all items in your cart
  - Individual item prices
  - Quantities for each item
  - **Subtotal**: Running total of all items
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Adjusting Quantities">
  Within the cart sidebar, you can:
  - **Increase quantity**: Click the + button
  - **Decrease quantity**: Click the - button
  - **Remove item**: Reduce quantity to 0 or use the remove button
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Emptying Cart">
  To remove all items at once:
  
  1. Open the cart sidebar
  2. Click **"Vaciar Carrito"** at the bottom
  3. Confirm the action
</Accordion>

<Note>
Your cart is stored locally in your browser. Items remain in your cart even if you close the browser, but they won't sync across different devices.
</Note>

## Placing an Order

### Checkout Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Your Cart">
    Open the cart sidebar and verify all items and quantities are correct.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login Required">
    Ensure you're logged in. If not, you'll be prompted to log in or create an account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Finalize Purchase">
    Click **"Finalizar Compra"** in the cart sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Order Confirmation">
    Upon successful order placement:
    - You'll receive a confirmation message
    - Your cart will be cleared
    - The order appears in your order history
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Make sure you have sufficient stock for all items. The system validates stock availability before processing your order.
</Tip>

## Order History

View all your past orders and their status.

### Accessing Order History

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to **"Mis Pedidos"** from the navigation menu
3. View your complete order history

### Order History Page

The order history displays:

<div className="grid grid-cols-1 gap-4">
  <div>
    - **Order ID**: Unique identifier for each order
    - **Date**: When the order was placed
    - **Summary**: Brief description of items
    - **Total**: Order amount
    - **Status**: Current order status (color-coded badge)
    - **Actions**: View details button
  </div>
</div>

### Viewing Order Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Order">
    Locate the order in your history list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Details">
    Click the **"Ver Detalle"** button for that order.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Information">
    A modal opens showing:
    - Complete list of products
    - Quantities ordered
    - Individual and total prices
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you have no orders yet, you'll see a message: "Aún no has realizado ninguna compra" with a link to return to the store.
</Note>

## Managing Your Profile

Customize your account information and preferences.

### Accessing Profile Settings

1. Log in to your account
2. Click on your **username** in the navigation bar
3. Select **"Mi Perfil"** or navigate to the profile page

### Profile Information

You can update the following:

<Accordion title="Personal Information">
  - **Name**: Your first name
  - **Apellido** (Last Name): Your surname
  - **Email**: Your email address (read-only, cannot be changed)
  - **Phone**: Contact telephone number
  - **Shipping Address**: Default delivery address
  
  Click **"Actualizar Perfil"** to save changes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Security Settings">
  ### Change Password
  
  To update your password:
  
  1. Enter your **Current Password**
  2. Enter your **New Password** (minimum 6 characters)
  3. Click **"Actualizar Contraseña"**
  
  <Note>
  Both fields are required to change your password successfully.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Account Deletion">
  ### Delete Your Account
  
  <Note type="warning">
  This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  </Note>
  
  To permanently delete your account:
  
  1. Scroll to the **"Zona de Peligro"** (Danger Zone) section
  2. Click **"Eliminar mi cuenta permanentemente"**
  3. Confirm the deletion
  
  **What happens when you delete your account:**
  - All your personal data is removed
  - Order history is permanently deleted
  - You cannot recover your account or data
  
  <Tip>
  Make sure you've downloaded any important order information before deleting your account.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Can't verify my email">
  **Solution:**
  1. Check your spam/junk folder
  2. Verify you entered the correct email during registration
  3. Try registering again if the verification link expired
  4. Contact support if issues persist
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Login fails after verification">
  **Solution:**
  - Ensure you clicked the verification link in your email
  - Verify you're using the correct email and password
  - Use the password recovery option if you forgot your password
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't add items to cart">
  **Solution:**
  - Check if the product has available stock
  - Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
  - Try refreshing the page
  - Clear browser cache and cookies
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Order failed to process">
  **Solution:**
  - Verify all items have sufficient stock
  - Ensure you're logged in
  - Check your internet connection
  - Try placing the order again
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't update my profile">
  **Solution:**
  - Verify all required fields are filled
  - Ensure your password meets minimum requirements (6 characters)
  - Check that you're entering your current password correctly when changing it
</Accordion>
